Stage 2: Extraction

Platforms shift surplus value from users to business customers

Monetization Mechanism

"TikTok is handing out giant teddy bears. But TikTok is not in the business of giving away giant teddy bears. TikTok is only going to funnel free attention to the people it wants to entrap until they are entrapped, then it will withdraw that attention and begin to monetize it."

Core Shift: Once users are locked in, platforms prioritize advertisers and business partners. Every "free" feature becomes a future revenue stream. User experience degrades as ads, promoted content, and algorithmic manipulation increase.

TikTok "Heating"

Secret button lets employees push videos to millions of feeds artificially. Used to court influencers and brands, creating manufactured virality that will later be withdrawn.

Amazon's Ad Wall

Search results become pay-to-play. Genuine relevance is replaced by sponsored listings, extracting value from sellers who must pay to be seen.
