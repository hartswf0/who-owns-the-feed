The "Enshittification" lifecycle of platforms

My concept of enshittification describes the predictable degradation of digital platforms through a three-stage process:

  1. Platforms serve users first to attract them
  2. Then they serve business customers to monetize
  3. Finally, they extract value from both groups until collapse
"First, they are good to their users; then they abuse their users to make things better for their business customers; finally, they abuse those business customers to claw back all the value for themselves. Then, they die." - Cory Doctorow

TikTok exemplifies this trajectory, beginning with user-friendly algorithms, then prioritizing advertisers, and now facing potential collapse as it extracts maximum value from all sides.
