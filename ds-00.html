The Enshittification Lifecycle

Cory Doctorow's Three-Stage Platform Death

1

COURTSHIP

Platform lavishes users with value. TikTok is "a free Adobe Premiere for teenagers" with uncannily good recommendations. Black Twitter uses hashtags to dominate trending topics.

2

EXTRACTION

Platform shifts surplus to business customers. TikTok's "heating" button artificially boosts partners. Amazon turns search into a pay-to-play ad wall.

3

DECAY

Platform claws back all value. "It becomes a useless pile of shit." Users flee or adapt as AI slop floods feeds and authentic culture evaporates.
